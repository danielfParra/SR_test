Time Limits

In each round, Player A will have {{Constants.TIME_PER_ROUND }} seconds time to choose a message. Player B will then have {{Constants.TIME_PER_ROUND }} seconds time to make a guess. After Player B makes a guess, both players will observe feedback for 5 seconds. Then, a new round starts.

Make sure that you stay within the {{Constants.TIME_PER_ROUND }} seconds allotted to you. If you don't, this will have consequences for your bonus payment.

Here is what happens if Player A does not send a message in time:

  • Player A will receive no bonus for sure for this round.
  • Player B will receive no message but can still make a guess.
  • Player B gets paid according to Player B's guess.

Here is what happens if Player B does not make a guess in time:

  • Player B's guess will be set equal to Player A's message. For example, if Player A advised "You should guess 5" and Player B fails to respond in time, Player B's guess will be 5.
  • Both players get paid according to Player B's guess.

If both players fail to stay within the time limit, both players will receive no bonus for the round.
